What We Do

Proterra Builders, Inc. is a California Close Corporation based in Hesperia, California that is a Disabled Veteran Business Enterprise and specializes in general building Class B. Services offered including preconstruction estimating, budgeting, general building construction and teaching.

Mission

Proterra Builders will offer Preconstruction services to its clients. Preconstruction services will include conceptual estimating, budgeting, and participation in design charrettes. We will also perform construction management services.

Vision

Build the highest quality construction, in the shortest timeframe, at the best value in the industry. Utilize current best practices, company values and critical success factors to achieve perfection. Provide program instruction centered on the construction industry i.e. estimating and bidding, construction law, concrete construction and masonry construction.

Values

  • Progress – always moving forward through agility, initiative, and creativity
  • Integrity – honesty in action and thought
  • Enjoyment – people are the core of our business. Happy people shatter the boundaries of expectations
  • Community – giving back to ourselves, our families, and where we live and work
  • Collaboration – making clients, subcontractors, suppliers and designers feel like we are an indispensable part of the team
